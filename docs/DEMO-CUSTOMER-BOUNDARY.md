# Demo Customer Authority Boundary

> Public-safe hackathon sample. This is not the store's operating policy,
> customer-service playbook, reply library, pricing, or production workflow.

The customer surface demonstrates only two synthetic examples:

1. **“My coral arrived dead.”** Teddy links to the local demo DOA form and
   collects sample evidence. A human remains responsible for any remedy.
2. **“Can I talk to the store team?”** The message enters the synthetic merchant
   attention queue for a person to review.

The demo deliberately omits real store rules, thresholds, exceptions, customer
segments, response templates, production URLs, and decision logic. Those are not
required to prove the product boundary: the robot performs the safe digital step;
the person retains the consequential decision.
